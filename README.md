# Tariqa

A family farm, community space, and living system being built near Valencia, Spain.

Tariqa means *the way* in Arabic — a Sufi term for the path of practice, not the destination.

We build with a bias toward sovereignty. Open source, regenerative, community-rooted.

**tariqa.land** | Valencia, 2026

---

## What's here

- `/skills` — reusable AI agent skills built for Anqa, open for anyone to use
- `/build-log` — how we built it, what worked, what didn't
- `/knowledge` — selected public notes on regenerative farming, self-hosted infrastructure, and sovereign systems

---

## Anqa

Anqa is our digital steward — an AI agent that manages tasks, captures decisions, processes documents, and maintains our knowledge base. She runs on Claude API, n8n, and plain markdown files. She belongs to Tariqa, not to any platform.

The skills in this repo are the ones she uses. Fork them, adapt them, build your own steward.
